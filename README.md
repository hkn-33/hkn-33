**Full warehouse management system**: sales, packing, shipping, deliveries and documents — MVP shipped in **2 weeks**, then extended with:

- 🧠 **AI agents** for automatic pricing and component matching
- 🔄 **Multi-channel sales sync** across marketplaces
- 📱 **Mobile scanner version** — barcode-driven warehouse workflows (inventory, bin picking, shelf visibility)
- 📸 **AI photo pipeline** — automated GPU background removal with 4 AI models, phone-to-browser capture via QR codes
